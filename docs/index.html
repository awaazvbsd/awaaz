üóìÔ∏è Phase-wise Pilot Rollout Plan

Structured approach to implementing AWAAZ in educational institutions

1

Phase 1: Engagement & Onboarding

Activities: Stakeholder meetings with school administration, counselors, teachers, and parents. Presentation of AWAAZ benefits and addressing concerns.

Duration: 2-3 weeks

Outcome: Buy-in from all stakeholders and formal agreement to pilot

2

Phase 2: Infrastructure Setup

Activities: Install necessary hardware (microphones, servers). Configure software systems and test data flow. Set up secure data storage and privacy protocols.

Duration: 3-4 weeks

Outcome: Fully operational system ready for testing

3

Phase 3: Training & Awareness

Activities: Train counselors on interpreting AI reports. Educate teachers on using the system. Conduct student awareness sessions on privacy and benefits.

Duration: 2-3 weeks

Outcome: All users comfortable with the system and ready for launch

4

Phase 4: Pilot Launch & Monitoring

Activities: Begin with small student cohort (50-100 students). Daily monitoring and feedback collection. Iterative improvements based on real-world usage.

Duration: 8-12 weeks

Outcome: Validated system with proven impact metrics

5

Phase 5: Full-Scale Deployment

Activities: Expand to entire school population. Continuous monitoring and optimization. Regular reporting to stakeholders on impact and outcomes.

Duration: Ongoing

Outcome: Comprehensive mental wellness support for all students
